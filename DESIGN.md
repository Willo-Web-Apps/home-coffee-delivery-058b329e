# Design DNA — Brewnest

## Visual Personality: warm-luxurious
## Layout: storytelling
## Typography: elegant-serif

### Color Palette
- Primary (Espresso): #2C1810 → oklch(0.18 0.04 38)
- Accent (Amber/Caramel): #C17B3A → oklch(0.62 0.12 52)
- Surface (Cream/Linen): #F5EFE6 → oklch(0.96 0.015 72)
- Text (Espresso dark): #1A0F08 → oklch(0.13 0.03 38)

### Font Pairing
- Display: Playfair Display — 400, 500, 700, 900 (editorial, richly serif)
- Body: Source Sans 3 — 300, 400, 600 (legible, warm, unhurried)

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Dark espresso hero & CTA** — deep brown/espresso backgrounds with cream text for dramatic contrast, cream sections in between; like flipping through a premium coffee magazine
2. **Pull quote sections** — large italic serif pull-quotes in amber break up the editorial flow, evoking artisan coffee packaging and independent café menus
3. **Asymmetric "origin story" layout** — story text offset to one side with a large decorative initial cap and an amber rule, giving a hand-crafted editorial feel rather than standard card grids
4. **Warm gradient hero** — layered radial glows in amber and brown at low chroma, with a noise/grain overlay, evoking morning light through a café window
